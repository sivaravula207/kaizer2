About Us

We Are Sinsan Pharma, A Healthcare Provider

We Sinsan Pharmaceuticals Pvt Ltd welcome you to a better tomorrow. Our endeavor is to evolve with the changing times to provide only the best to our customers. The pharmaceutical industry, over the years, has seen immense growth. However, since our inception in 2007, we have proved time and again, that our products are the best in the market. Sinsan Pharmaceuticals has become one of the leading pharmaceutical companies in India; thanks to the perseverance and diligent efforts of our founders and directors, supported by a family of more than 500 family members.

With an ever-expanding team of enthusiastic youngsters and a constant thirst of achieving milestones, we are on a mission to become one of the best Indian Pharma companies with a global presence. Within a short span of time, we have expanded our operations PAN India and have an arsenal of more than 500 dedicated field force.

  • We are dedicated to spreading smiles across our customers and making sure they get the best in patient-care.
  • Our commitment to quality is the reason we are trusted by Doctors all across the country.
  • Our network is ever-growing; thanks to the trust that we have built based on our existence so far.

For many years now, Sinsan Pharmaceuticals Pvt. Ltd. has been making the world a healthier place, with our innovative approach towards modern medicine and an understanding of today’s millennial. With the change in the present lifestyle, health-related problems like diabetes and high blood-pressure are not uncommon. This has persuaded Sinsan Pharma to work towards developing effective, yet low-priced medicines. World-class oral hypoglycemic drugs and anti-hypertensive drugs are part of our endeavor to make global-standard medicines available across the world at local cost.
